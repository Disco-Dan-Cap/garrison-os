// The Solution

// How the architecture works
Institutional leads anchor the round at agreed terms. Disco's vetted retail community co-invests on identical terms — same allocation, same cap table position. The lead's commitment is the quality gate by design. Retail only enters rounds where institutional conviction is already priced in. Deal selection is not an operations problem — it's an architectural property.

// The Blend Feature

Diversify without selling.

Punch Bowl's blending lets investors gain exposure to deals they missed — and share exposure from deals they got into — without anyone needing to liquidate. Composition replaces transaction.

←
Missed the round?
A current holder blends a slice of their position into yours. You gain proportional exposure to a deal you couldn't get into. They retain their core stake.
→
Got in early?
Held the round others wanted? Blend a portion of your sought-after position into another investor's portfolio. Diversify without divesting.
⇄
No one sells.
Smart contracts handle the math. Hybrid NFT instruments emerge. Liquidity flows through composition, not forced exits.
Programmable cap tables make this possible. The Punch Bowl makes it accessible.
