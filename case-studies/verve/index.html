The ceiling fan, reimagined as a smart-home hub.

Verve was a project I worked on in collaboration with Emerson Technologies. The goal was to design a “smart” ceiling fan that could be integrated into an old or modern home. Ceiling fans are a prime location for many smart-home technologies because of their raised, center location in a room.

01 — The idea

Safe, central, and smart.

Verve features three foam blades, a child monitor / security camera, and a Bluetooth speaker ring. The foam blades were inspired by the foam used in Nerf products — developed after my research found a trend of kids hurting hands or hitting heads on fan blades while playing on their beds. The fan was designed to accommodate children from a very young age up to young adulthood.
